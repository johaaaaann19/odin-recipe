Pork Chop Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rib Chop
  • Olive oil
  • Corn
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper

Procedure

  1. Remove your pork chops from the refrigerator and season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. If you have 5-10 minutes to spare, allow your chops to rest and come to room temperature.
  3. STo cook on the stovetop, heat a little bit of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the remaining tablespoon of butter, the minced garlic, and chopped thyme to the skillet.
